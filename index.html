About

Welcome to The Family Hub Café!

The Family Hub Café is a space for families with little ones. Our vibrant and welcoming space is designed to bring families together, while providing a fun and safe environment for kids to play, learn, and grow.

Play

Play.

Our play area is designed to stimulate imagination and curiosity, with:

  • Soft play equipment and climbing structures
  • Sensory play areas and imagination stations
  • Storytime and music sessions
  • Events and other interactive activities for parents and kids
  • Outdoor play space with weather permitting

The play area is free of charge for the users of the café. Parents or guardians supervise their own children at all times and remain responsible for their safety and behaviour while using the facilities.

Connect

Connect.

We understand that parenting can be isolating, which is why we've created a space where you can meet fellow parents, share experiences, and make new friends. Our comfortable seating areas and outdoor spaces are perfect for catching up over some food and drink, while keeping a watchful eye on your little ones.

A supportive community for expecting parents

  • A safe space to connect with other expecting parents
  • Prenatal yoga and parenting workshops
  • Baby-friendly events and activities to prepare for parenthood

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Do I need to pay to use the play area?
No, the play area is free of charge for the users of the café.
Do you offer private hire?
Yes, we offer private hire, please contact us for party packages and availability.
Is it stroller friendly?
Yes, to get to the café there is a stroller friendly pathway to the right of the building complex.
Is there parking nearby?
Yes, there are various parking options available within a short walk from the café – free on street and metered parking options.
