About

The Family Hub Café

The Family Hub Café is a parent-supervised children’s play centre offering a series of parent/child activities focused on the physical, emotional and social development of young children.

Our open play structures and safe environment encourage movement, imagination and group interaction. Parents or guardians must supervise their own children at all times while using the play centre.

0–7 years Parent supervision Development-focused play
Play centre

A safe space to explore, move and imagine

Our large, open play area is designed to entertain and stimulate children through movement, exploration and creativity. From soft play zones to imaginative corners and climbing structures, we create an environment where children can build confidence and social skills through group play.

All activity areas are parent-supervised. Parents or guardians must actively supervise their own children at all times and are fully responsible for their safety and behaviour while using the facilities.

Café

Family-friendly food & coffee

Alongside the play centre, our café serves simple, fresh and family-friendly food. We offer breakfast and lunch options, snacks for little ones, artisan coffee, smoothies and juices — with allergy-conscious and child-friendly choices available.

Whether you’re joining us for a play session or just stopping by for a coffee, The Family Hub Café is a warm place for families to gather.

Play

Children’s play area

Our play area is open throughout the day for free play. Children can explore, climb, imagine and enjoy the space, while parents relax in the café with coffee, food or snacks.

We do not run scheduled classes or monthly programmes. Instead, we offer a relaxed, open environment designed for natural play, social interaction and creativity, with parents supervising their own children at all times.

FAQ

Questions parents often ask

Do I need to book in advance?
We recommend booking in advance, especially on weekends and during busy periods, to ensure a space for your child.
Is the play centre supervised?
The play area is not supervised by staff. Parents or guardians must supervise their own children at all times and are fully responsible for their safety and behaviour while using the facilities.
What ages is The Family Hub Café suitable for?
The play centre is designed primarily for children aged 0–7. Please ask us if you have specific questions about your child’s needs.
Can I bring my own food?
To keep the space safe and consistent for all families, we ask that you enjoy food and drinks from our café only, except for specific medical or allergy-related requirements.
