Eat • Play • Connect

Family Hub Café is a perfect spot for everyone. Whether you are a family with little ones, a couple, or enjoying time solo, our vibrant and welcoming space is designed to bring people together.

We provide a fun and safe environment where children can play, learn & explore, and everyone can enjoy delicious food and company.

About us

We are a family who made the leap to move to Portugal. We know firsthand how challenging it can be to integrate into a new community and meet new people. We missed the feeling of having “a village” around you, especially as new parents. We also understand the struggle of trying to grab a bite to eat with a little one in tow: the limited options and the constant juggle to keep everyone happy.

At Family Hub Café, our vision is to create a space where children can play and explore, while adults unwind and enjoy good food and company — a spot where everyone can dine together stress-free and feel included.

Eat

Our menu features a range of healthy and tasty options sourced from local suppliers. We strive to use organic ingredients where possible. We have options for all members of the family.

Play

Our play area is designed to stimulate imagination and curiosity, with:

  • Soft play equipment and climbing structures
  • Sensory play areas and imagination stations
  • Outdoor play space (weather permitting)
  • Events and other interactive activities for parents and children
  • We aim to host various events and interactive activities for parents and children throughout the year

Connect

We understand that parenting can be isolating, which is why we’ve created a space where you can meet fellow parents, share experiences, and make new friends. Our comfortable seating areas and outdoor spaces are perfect for catching up over food and a drink while keeping a watchful eye on your little ones.

At Family Hub Café, we’re creating a supportive community for expecting parents. Join us for a safe space to connect with others. We aim to host various activities and events for expecting parents throughout the year.

FAQ

Cost of using play area
It costs €5 to use the play area per child (siblings go free)
Do you offer private hire?
Yes, we offer private hire. Please contact us for party packages and availability.
Is it stroller friendly?
Yes, there is a stroller-friendly pathway to the right of the building complex.
Is there parking nearby?
Yes, there are parking options within a short walk, including free on-street and metered parking.
