Eat • Play • Connect

Family Hub Café is a perfect spot for everyone. Whether you are a family with little ones, a couple, or enjoying time solo, our vibrant and welcoming space is designed to bring people together. We provide a fun and safe environment where children can play, learn & explore, and everyone can enjoy delicious food and company

About us

We are a family who made the leap to move to Portugal, we know firsthand how challenging it can be to integrate into a new community and meet new people. We missed the feeling of having "a village" around you, especially as new parents. We also understand the struggle of trying to grab a bite to eat with a little one in tow, the limited options, the constant juggle to keep everyone happy. At Family Hub Cafe, our vision is to create a space where children can play and explore, while adults unwind, enjoy good food and company. A spot where everyone could dine together stress-free, and feel included

Eat

Our menu features a range of healthy and tasty options sourced from local suppliers. We strive to use organic ingredients were possible. We have options for all members of the family

Play

Our play area is designed to stimulate imagination and curiosity, with

  • Soft play equipment and climbing structures
  • Sensory play areas and imagination stations
  • Outdoor play space (weather permitting)
  • Events and other interactive activities for parents and kids
  • We aim to host various events and interactive activities for parents and children throughout the year

Connect

We understand that parenting can be isolating, which is why we've created a space where you can meet fellow parents, share experiences, and make new friends. Our comfortable seating areas and outdoor spaces are perfect for catching up over food and a drink, while keeping a watchful eye on your little ones. At Family Hub Cafe, we're creating a supportive community for expecting parents. Join us for a safe space to connect with others. We aim to host various activities and events aimed at expecting parents throughtout the year

A supportive community for expecting parents

  • A safe space to connect with other expecting parents
  • Prenatal yoga and parenting workshops
  • Baby-friendly events and activities to prepare for parenthood
